110 kV Oil-Immersed Transformer

The 110 kV oil-immersed power transformer is engineered for utility substations, grid interconnection points, and large industrial supply systems where long-term reliability, stable voltage regulation, thermal stability, and dependable insulation performance are critical to continuous operation.

Request a Quote

Product Details

This 110 kV oil-immersed transformer is designed for high-availability grid duty rather than voltage class alone. Its liquid-immersed core-and-coil system uses insulating oil for both dielectric strength and heat dissipation, helping maintain stable electrical and thermal behavior under continuous service. The platform is suitable for public utilities, urban substations, and industrial load centers where interruption costs are high and reliability targets are strict.

Project configurations can be tailored around cooling arrangements, tank design, and de-energized or on-load tap-changing strategies according to network regulation requirements. With a focus on moisture control, winding mechanical strength, and validated factory testing such as impulse and temperature-rise tests, this series supports dependable long-term operation, reduced outage risk, and stronger lifecycle value for international EPCs and power asset owners.
