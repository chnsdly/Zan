S22 is typically treated as the high-efficiency tier within the S13/S20/S22 sealed oil-immersed distribution family. Its value proposition centers on stronger loss control under real operating conditions, which becomes increasingly important for projects with long annual service hours and strict operating-expenditure targets. For such applications, improved efficiency directly contributes to lower energy cost and stronger long-term asset performance.

The series is an appropriate fit for modern industrial parks, smart distribution networks, and ESG-driven infrastructure programs that prioritize lifecycle energy outcomes. As with all distribution transformers, model name alone does not define guaranteed loss, impedance, or vector group values, so procurement decisions should be based on the exact technical offer and certified test documentation. In practical sales terms, S22 is best positioned as a premium energy-efficient sealed oil-immersed distribution solution.
